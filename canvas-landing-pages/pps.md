## About

In this module, students will learn the fundamentals of data visualization using Python's Pandas library. They will explore best practices for creating effective visualizations, including how to choose the appropriate chart type for different data sets. Through hands-on exercises, students will implement various visualizations such as bar charts, line charts, scatterplots, and histograms, gaining the skills necessary to effectively communicate data insights.
